# Event Importance Rules

Every `TimelineEvent` must be assigned an `EventImportance` level and an exact `importance_score` (0-100).
This score dictates the visual hierarchy on the Dashboard, Timeline, and future Notifications.

## Importance Levels

- CRITICAL (75-100): Immediate action required today. Deadline imminent.
- HIGH (50-74): Action may be needed soon. Highly relevant tomorrow or very recent.
- MEDIUM (25-49): Worth knowing about. Happening next week.
- LOW (0-24): Informational. Historical events or > 2 weeks away.

| Any               | Any                | Far Future (> 2 weeks away) | 5     | LOW              |

## Conflict Resolution
If an event qualifies for multiple rules (e.g., IPO Listing is tomorrow AND it's a high-profile IPO), the higher score wins.

## Dashboard Filtering Rule
The "Today/Upcoming" high-density widget on the Dashboard will ONLY display events with `importance_score >= 50` (HIGH or CRITICAL).
Events below 50 will only appear in the chronological Timeline view or specific detail views.
